Welcome to Perfume Direct - The UK’s premier online fragrance & beauty store.

Stocking a range of specially selected perfumes, aftershaves & gift sets from luxury brands such as Hugo Boss, Gucci, Armani, Paco Rabanne, Calvin Klein, DKNY, Jimmy Choo & many more. Our customer-focused approach means that our business is built around you. We set out to provide the best products, at the best price with the best customer care package on the market. We are extremely proud of our fast, reliable delivery network, which means all orders will be shipped out in lightning-quick time from our state-of-the-art warehouse located in Manchester, UK.

Customer focused approach

At Scentsation, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to providing a seamless shopping experience, ensuring that every fragrance lover finds their perfect scent with ease. From carefully curating a diverse selection of premium perfumes to offering detailed scent descriptions, we strive to make your fragrance journey enjoyable and effortless. Our dedicated customer support team is always ready to assist you, whether you need recommendations, have questions, or require personalized guidance. We believe that shopping for perfumes should be a luxurious and satisfying experience. That’s why we focus on quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. Every order is handled with care, packaged beautifully, and delivered with excellence. Your happiness is our priority, and we take pride in creating an experience that makes you return for more. At Scentsation, you’re not just buying a fragrance—you’re becoming part of a community that celebrates elegance, individuality, and the art of fine perfumery.
